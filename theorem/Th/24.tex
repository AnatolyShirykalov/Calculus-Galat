\[\forall\ \ol x = \ve xM\in U\pau f(x_1,\ldots,x_{M_0-1},x_{M_0}\ldots,x_M)=f(x_1,\ldots,x_{M_0-1},x_{M_0}^0,\ldots,x_M^0).\]
